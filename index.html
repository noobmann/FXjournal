📚 How to Use FX Journal for Profitable Trading
🎯 Getting Started
  1. Set up your account: Go to Accounts and add your trading account with initial balance.
  2. Configure your settings: Set your timezone, currency, and trading style in Settings.
  3. Define your rules: Add your trading rules in the Rules section to track discipline.
  4. Customize sessions: Adjust market sessions if needed to match your trading hours.
📝 Logging Trades Properly
  1. Pre-trade checklist: Always fill out the checklist before entering a trade to ensure you're following your plan.
  2. Record entry details: Enter pair, direction, session, setup type, entry price, stop loss, and take profit.
  3. Add position size: Use the Position Calculator tool to determine proper lot size based on your risk.
  4. Update after exit: Once the trade closes, update the exit price and add notes about what happened.
  5. Journal every trade: Write detailed analysis in the Journal tab - what worked, what didn't, emotional state.
💡 Using Tools for Better Decisions
  • Market Hours Tracker: Check active sessions and overlaps to trade during high-volume periods.
  • Position Size Calculator: Never guess lot sizes - calculate based on your stop loss and risk percentage.
  • Gain/Loss Calculator: Plan your trades by calculating potential profit/loss before entering.
📊 Analyzing Your Performance
  1. Review Dashboard daily: Check your equity curve, win rate, and recent trades.
  2. Study Analytics weekly: Identify which pairs, sessions, and setups work best for you.
  3. Use AI Report: Get unbiased analysis of your trading patterns and personalized recommendations.
  4. Track consistency: Look at the calendar heatmap to see your trading consistency.
🧠 Building Profitable Habits
  • Risk management first: Never risk more than 1-2% per trade. Use the calculator religiously.
  • Trade your edge: Only take setups that match your proven strategy. Avoid FOMO trades.
  • Review losers carefully: Losing trades teach more than winners. Journal them thoroughly.
  • Track emotions: Note your emotional state before, during, and after each trade.
  • Follow your rules: The Rules tracker will alert you when you break your own guidelines.
  • Be consistent: Trade the same sessions, same pairs, same setups until you're profitable.
  • Take breaks: After 2 consecutive losses, stop trading. Review and reset.
⚠ Common Mistakes to Avoid
  • ❌ Overtrading - taking too many trades or trading outside your session
  • ❌ Revenge trading - trying to win back losses immediately
  • ❌ Moving stop losses - always respect your predefined risk
  • ❌ Skipping journal entries - incomplete data leads to wrong conclusions
  • ❌ Ignoring the dashboard warnings - pay attention to declining metrics
  • ❌ Trading without a setup - every trade must have a valid reason
💡 Pro Tip: The key to profitability is not finding a "holy grail" strategy - it's consistent execution of a simple edge, proper risk management, and continuous improvement through honest self-review. Use this journal religiously, and you'll see patterns that transform your trading.

ℹ️ How to Use

Position sizing is critical for risk management. Never guess your lot size - always calculate based on your stop loss distance.

  • Enter your account balance and the percentage you want to risk (typically 1-2%)
  • Input your planned entry price and stop loss in pips
  • The calculator determines the exact lot size to keep your risk within limits
  • For JPY pairs, pip calculation is automatic (0.01 instead of 0.0001)
  • For Gold/Silver, calculations adjust for their unique pip values
💡 Rule: If the calculated position seems too large, reduce your risk % or widen your stop loss. Never force a trade with improper sizing.
